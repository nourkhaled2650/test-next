Dr. Ashraf Sheta biography

Possessing practical entrepreneurial experience is a crucial dimension in the career of Dr. Sheta. He was the GM of the Shetatex Company for weaving from 1990 till May 2012. With more than 25 years of experience in the field of textiles. For the academic dimension, Dr. Sheta authored and co-authored more than 40 teaching cases addressing the Egyptian context.

He iDr. Sheta is currently working as an adjunct assistant professor for entrepreneurship and family business at the American university. His academic, and nonacademic work is diversified with a main concentration in the fields of entrepreneurship, small business development, strategic planning, innovation and family business.

as the winner of the second prize award of the AUC/Emerald case writing competition ,2020 He is the winner of the EFMD award for case writing in the field of family business ,2019.

He is the winner of the silver award Stellar Teaching and Activities Recognition (STAR) 2018/2019 to recognize faculty who adequately demonstrate the extent and nature of alignment between their teaching and other activities with the AUC business School's vision, mission, themes and focus on Internationalization and Arab relevance.

He is the winner of two Performance awards for contributions, from the board of trustees of the AUC for the academic years 2018, 2019. >He is the winner of the adjunct faculty teaching excellence award at the AUC for the academic year 2016. He developed and delivered new courses for the first time in Egypt including: family business, Innovation and technology, Corporate entrepreneurship, and Entrepreneurial management.

For the consultancy and training dimension, Sheta is an expert in the fields of Entrepreneurship, small business development, Innovation, Strategy, and Family business. Sheta’ has worked with prominent organizations in the field of training and consultancy; GIZ, Transparency international, European union, USAID, ILO, international organization of migration IOM, Executive Education Executive education at the AUC and Women entrepreneurship and leadership program WEL developed by Wharton school of business and financed by Goldman Sachs. He also won the certificate of performance excellence from the AUC executive education for the years 2014,2015,2016,2017.

Addressing the public service dimension, Dr. Sheta is former president and board member of the Middle East council for small business and entrepreneurship MAKSABY an affiliate of the international council for small business ICSB with a strategic agenda of promoting and supporting entrepreneurship and small business in the MENA region.

He is also a member of the ICSB academy instructors in the field of entrepreneurship and innovation with an agenda to promote entrepreneurship education internationally.

He was the vice president of the Egyptian junior business association EJB and was responsible for developing the Egyptian national business agenda in partnership with center for international private enterprises CIPE for three consecutive years till November 2009 tackling 14 different industries in Egypt.

He was also a founder of the Egyptian textiles technology center, and member of the textile trade center steering committee.

Vice president of the Egyptian junior business association till 2009.
