About Us

AS is a privately held company working in the fields of consultancy and training,
Our scope of work includes five main strategic areas: Entrepreneurship, Corporate, Innovation, Small and medium Enterprise, and
Family Business. These areas were chosen carefully to cover the corporate journey from the embryonic till the maturity stage.
We are proud of our diversified team who possess both practical, and academically qualified background. This helped our
organization to attain a core competence manifested in is its ability to design, and implement customized programs for our
customers. As well as the fact that all of our programs are mainly concerned about practical outcomes, which can create value for
our customers on both the short, and long term run.
